Welcome!

This project is about understanding you and your online data better, and visualizing what Google and other services on the internet know about you. It gives you a deeper perspective on your online actions and what personal data and identifying information about you is available by producing visual representation.

The people who choose to participate and provide their data will get this deeper understanding and awareness of their online data that you may not be aware of otherwise. This will open participants eyes to what data is available to online organisations. It gives a deeper appreciation and understanding of your digital footprint, your identity. If you'd like to continue and follow the steps to know more about how your online data represents you, please hit the enter button below.
